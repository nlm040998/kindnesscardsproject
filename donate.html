Donations

This is a non-profit organisation run by me alone, meaning I am reliant on donations to keep sending Kindness Packages. I have a Paypal Pool where any amount of money is appreciated, and an Amazon Wish List which gives items that are perfect for the packages. Everything donated goes straight back into the Kindness Cards Project and helps those who may need a little extra kindness in their life right now.
